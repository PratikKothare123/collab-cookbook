# 🍗 Chicken Curry (Desi Style 😋🔥)

- **Cuisine:** Indian 🇮🇳
- **Author:** [@PratikKothare123](https://github.com/PratikKothare123)
- **Mood:** Jab bhook lage aur dil bole "Chicken chahiye!" 😂

---

## 🛒 Ingredients (Samaan Ready Rakho 👨‍🍳)

- 500g chicken 🍗 (cleaned & cut — hero of the story)
- 2 onions 🧅 (finely chopped — aansu guaranteed 😭)
- 2 tomatoes 🍅 (pureed)
- 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste 🧄
- 2 tbsp oil 🛢️
- 1 tsp turmeric powder (haldi power 💛)
- 1 tsp red chili powder 🌶️ (spicy level = attitude level)
- 1 tsp garam masala ✨
- Salt 🧂 (as per your dil ❤️)
- Fresh coriander 🌿 (for Instagram look 📸)

---

## 👨‍🍳 Steps (Chef Mode ON 🔥)

1. Pan garam karo aur oil daalo — jaise life me energy add karte ho 😎.
2. Onion daalo aur golden brown hone tak bhuno (patience test 😅).
3. Ginger-garlic paste add karo — ab kitchen me asli fragrance aayega 🤤.
4. Tomato puree add karo aur tab tak cook karo jab tak oil alag na ho (ye important hai boss!).
5. Haldi, mirchi, salt aur garam masala add karo — masala = magic ✨.
6. Chicken pieces daalo aur achhe se mix karo — sabko equal respect 😄.
7. Medium flame pe 20–25 min cook karo jab tak chicken soft na ho jaye.
8. Coriander se garnish karo — ab dish ban gayi *MasterChef level* 😎🍗.

---

## 💡 Pro Tips (Secret Chef Gyaan 🤫)

- Jaldi mat karo — chicken bhi relationship jaisa hai, time do 😄.
- Zyada pani mat daalo warna curry soup ban jayegi 😂.
- Roti, rice ya direct spoon se bhi kha sakte ho (no judgement 😎).

---

## 🍽️ Serving Suggestion

Serve hot with:
- Roti 🫓
- Rice 🍚
- Ya phir dost ke plate se chura ke 😜

**Warning:** Ek baar khaoge toh roz demand aayegi 😄🔥
